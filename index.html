About Me

My name is Greg Trahan. Currently, I am enrolled in the University of Minnesota Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp, and I have aspirations to become a full-stack developer. Prior to the bootcamp, I worked as a Research Technologist at the Mayo Clinic studying gene therapies for collagen disorders, and then as a Research Associate at Bio-Techne. In my free time, I am an avid reader of science fiction and fantasy novels, with Brandon Sanderson being one of my favorite authors. My two cats and I live with three housemates, where we are frequently found playing pinochle, board games, or watching the Great British Baking Show.
